Great Vacations

Florida
Florida is my next great trip. I am going to thoroughly enjoy going there. My girlfriend and I will be going to Universal Studios. There's also a place called Mario Andretti Go-Kart Racing. I intend to win many races against my girlfriend instead of watching the Super Bowl.
Yosemite
Yosemite has everything anyone who loves to hike and camp could ask for. The park is the most popular in the United States and renowned worldwide. The waterfalls, cliffs, sequoias and wildlife are breathtaking. Unfortunately it is also popular and can get as busy as Disneyland.
New York
New York is full of a shocking amount of urban sights, from the Empire State Building and Central Park to One World Trade and Wall Street. There is an incredible amount of history and museums to take in as well as culture all over the city. It is Americana at it's height, pun intended.
