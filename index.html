Chicken

I love chicken. It's what's for dinner. I can prepare it in a multitude of ways like fried, baked, boiled, or flame grilled. Smoked chicken is easily the best though. My girlfriend loves when I make chicken because I use amazing spices like lemon pepper, paprika and garlic salt as well as parsely and oregano to add flavor. Adding chicken to any dish immediately improves that dish. Have pasta? Add Chicken. Have a burger? Chicken burgers are healthier. Have veggies? Adding chicken adds protein. Chicken is likely the best meat.
